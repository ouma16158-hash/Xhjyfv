This is a complete architectural blueprint for a Two-Way Professional Matching Platform.
Imagine a system where, instead of searching through thousands of random job posts, a "Match" only appears if both the Job Seeker and the Company are exactly what the other is looking for. It works like a high-end dating app, but for careers.
1. The Job Seeker (User) Setup
The User must complete two distinct pages: Identity and Search Filters.
Page A: User Info (The "Who I Am")
This data is what Companies use to find the User.
 * Biometrics:
   * Full Name: [Text Input]
   * Gender: [Dropdown: Male, Female, Other]
   * Age: [Numeric Input]
 * Media Portfolio:
   * Profile Photo: [Single Image Upload]
   * Video Intros: [Upload up to 10 videos, max 3 minutes each]. Users can name each video.
   * Document Vault: [Upload up to 10 PDF/Docs]. Users can name each (e.g., "Degree," "CV").
 * Education & Professional Major:
   * Major Category: [Dropdown: Engineering & Tech, Business & Admin, Health & Sciences, Arts & Design, Humanities/Social]
   * Sub-Major Selection: [Conditional Dropdown based on Major]:
     * If Engineering: Civil & Structural, Software & Data, Electrical, Mechanical.
     * If Business: Finance & Accounting, Marketing, HR, Operations.
     * If Health: Clinical, Laboratory, Public Health, Admin.
   * Education Level: [Dropdown: Certificate, Diploma, Bachelor’s, Master’s, PhD]
 * Logistics & Pay:
   * Current Location: [Text: City/Country]
   * Relocation: [Toggle: Yes / No]
   * Night Shift Availability: [Toggle: Yes / No]
   * Minimum Salary: [Numeric: The absolute lowest pay they will accept]
Page B: User Preferences (The "What I Want")
This filters which Companies appear on the User's Dashboard.
 * Workplace Mode: [Checkboxes: Remote, Hybrid, On-site]
 * Company Size: [Checkboxes: Startup, Mid-size, Corporate]
 * Target Industry: [Multi-select: Pick any from the Major Categories]
 * Recency (The "Today" Filter): [Button: Only show jobs posted in the last 24 hours]
2. The Company (Employer) Setup
The Company fills out these pages for every job they post.
Page A: Company Info (The "What the Job Is")
This data is what Users use to find the Job.
 * Identity: Company Name, Logo, and Industry.
 * Work Mode: [Selection: Remote, Hybrid, or On-site]
 * Salary: [Numeric: The Maximum budget for this role]
 * Shift Type: [Toggle: Day Shift or Night Shift]
 * Location: [Text: Office Address]
 * Relocation Perk: [Toggle: We provide relocation assistance: Yes / No]
 * Post Date: [Automatic Timestamp]
Page B: Company Preferences (The "Ideal Candidate")
This filters which Users appear on the Company's Dashboard.
 * Demographics:
   * Gender Preference: [Dropdown: Male, Female, or Any]
   * Minimum Age: [Numeric: e.g., Must be at least 21]
 * Qualifications:
   * Required Major & Sub-Major: [Must match the User’s selections exactly]
   * Min Education Level: [Dropdown: e.g., Must have at least a Bachelor’s]
 * Logistics:
   * Relocation Requirement: [Toggle: Candidate must be willing to move]
   * Night Shift Requirement: [Toggle: Candidate must work nights]
 * Recency Filter: [Button: Only show candidates who applied in the last 24 hours]
3. The Dashboard Matching Tables
The system compares the Info of one side against the Preferences of the other.
Table 1: User Dashboard (Seeing Companies)
| What the User Sees | Matching Requirement |
|---|---|
| The Job Card | Company Info must match User Preferences. |
| Salary Display | Company Max Budget \ge User Min Salary. |
| Work Mode | Company Mode must be in the User’s Preferred list. |
| "Today" Filter | Only shows jobs where Post Date = Today. |
Table 2: Company Dashboard (Seeing Candidates)
| What the Company Sees | Matching Requirement |
|---|---|
| Candidate Profile | User Info must match Company Preferences. |
| Age/Gender | User Age \ge Company Min Age AND Gender matches. |
| Major/Skills | User Sub-Major must match Company Requirement. |
| Video/Docs | All 10 videos and 10 docs are visible for review. |
| "Today" Filter | Only shows users who applied today. |
4. Why This Works (The "Secret Sauce")
This platform eliminates "spam." A user never sees a job they aren't qualified for or that pays too low. A company never sees a candidate who doesn't meet their age, gender, or education requirements.
By adding the 10 videos (3 mins each) and 10 named documents, the company can "meet" the candidate virtually before ever scheduling an interview, making the process 10x faster.
